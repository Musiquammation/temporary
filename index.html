Welcome to Planify

This application automatically schedules the tasks you need to complete based on when you are available to work.

To do this, drag your finger across the grid to create a time slot (your availability). You can then choose whether or not to set preferences for the types of tasks you prefer to work on.

By pressing the + button at the bottom right, you can add the tasks you need to complete.

Finally, the Place tasks button automatically places your tasks on the grid so you don’t have to decide what to do next.
